Systems and Methods for Aircraft-Profile-Based Force-Feedback Control for Simulated Rotary-Wing Aircraft- Patent Pending

While evaluating a force feedback joystick, I identified an unsolved problem in the flight simulation community: no universal method existed for implementing force-trim and autopilot logic for helicopter controls. I researched real-world helicopter autopilot and trim system documentation, then designed and built a C# application by collaborating with AI tooling. It bridges simulator telemetry to force feedback hardware.

C# SimConnect AI

Bridges almost any helicopter telemetry data from simulators to force feedbase joystick bases.

  • Capable of reading autopilot inputs, trim inputs, and correct force trim release logic

PC 90 Jet Engine Turbofan Case

A fully 3D-printable small form factor PC case modeled after the GE-90 turbofan jet engine.

Tag Tag

The build features a 200mm fan housed in a cowling proportionally scaled to the real engine's bypass ratio and diameter. Cooling is handled by a custom external liquid cooling loop with dual 280mm radiators, mounted behind printable covers designed to resemble airport blast fences. Design and engineering challenges in Fusion 360 were solved through a combination of AI tooling, independent research, and domain knowledge from building open-source 3D Printers.
